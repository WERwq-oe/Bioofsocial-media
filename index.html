Frequently Asked Questions

What is AvThR?

AvThR is a free AI-powered platform that generates comprehensive reports and interactive quizzes on any topic. It's designed to help students, educators, and professionals create quality educational content instantly.

How do I generate a report?

Simply enter your topic, select the desired length and formatting options, then click "Generate Report". The AI will create a comprehensive report that you can download in multiple formats.

Can I download the generated content?

Yes! You can download reports and quizzes in PDF, DOCX, and HTML formats. All downloads maintain proper formatting and pagination.

Is AvThR free to use?

Yes, AvThR is completely free to use. No registration or payment required. We believe in making quality educational tools accessible to everyone.

What quiz formats are available?

You can generate multiple choice questions (MCQs), one-word answers, and interactive flashcards. You can select multiple formats for a single quiz.
